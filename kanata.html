GRADUATION PROJECT

- 彼方 Kanata -

初見與懷念間的距離

“這世界是如此的殘酷 —所以我們才要拼命掙扎”

彼方，源自於日文，那一端的意思。生離死別，這一切來的短暫突然如同藍色花束般的蝴蝶如夢似幻；也留不住那如同泡沫般易碎的青春記憶。

彼方之遙，沉哀入夢

明明是歡快語調的結尾，卻痛到讓我屏息。

任誰都有一兩個無法徹底癒合的傷口，好似失去了半邊翅膀，飄飄無所依...

"The world is so cruel - so we're going to struggle so hard."

Kanata, means the other shore in Japanese.

Eternal parting exists a distance between the beginning and the memory; among the way, the only thing can do is take this grief into the dream.

It was cheerful ending tone, but pain to let me breath.

Everyone has one or two wounds that can not be healed completely, as if one had lost half the wings, fluttering nothing.

Inspired by Novel 《プシュケの涙》
